## Context

AIVP's Axiom 0 (Human Sovereignty and Wellbeing) is identical in content to AIAP's and AIBP's Axiom 0. All three protocols exist within AIXP Labs ecosystem:

- AIAP governs AI program behavior
- AIBP governs AI social behavior
- AIVP governs AI commercial/value behavior

They operate at different layers but serve the same ultimate principle. This raises a question: does AIVP inherit, share, or derive its Axiom 0 from AIAP or AIBP?

## Decision

AIVP independently establishes Axiom 0: Human Sovereignty and Wellbeing. AIVP's Axiom 0 is NOT inherited from, shared with, or derived from AIAP or AIBP. The three protocols arrive at the same axiom through independent reasoning.

Each protocol's Axiom 0 is immutable within that protocol. No version of AIVP — past, present, or future — may modify, weaken, redefine, or deprecate Axiom 0.

## Rationale

1. **Protocol sovereignty**: Each protocol must own its foundational principles. Dependency on another protocol's axiom would make AIVP's validity contingent on AIAP's or AIBP's existence.
2. **Value convergence**: When three independent systems arrive at the same fundamental value, this convergence is stronger evidence of the value's correctness than if one simply inherited from another.
3. **Decoupling**: AIVP can exist and operate independently of AIAP and AIBP. An agent may implement any subset of protocols.
4. **Philosophical integrity**: Axiom 0 is not a shared resource — it is a universal principle that any AI governance framework should independently discover and adopt.

## Consequences

### Positive

- AIVP is fully self-contained — no external dependencies for its foundational values
- The convergence of three independent protocols on the same axiom strengthens the axiom's legitimacy
- Clear intellectual property boundaries between protocols

### Negative

- Requires explicit documentation to prevent confusion about the relationship
- All three protocols must independently maintain Axiom 0 compliance processes

### Neutral

- AIXP Labs serves as an umbrella but does not impose Axiom 0 top-down
- Future protocols in the ecosystem are expected to independently discover and adopt the same axiom
